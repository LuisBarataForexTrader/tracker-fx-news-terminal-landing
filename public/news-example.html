Trump Says Iran Agreed to Suspend Nuclear Program Indefinitely

📅 April 17, 2026 · 15:33 UTC
🏷 Geopolitics · Macro
⚡ Trading News Terminal
USD ▲ GOLD ▼ OIL ▼ EUR/USD ▼

President Trump announced that Iran has agreed to suspend its nuclear program indefinitely, with no time limit on the suspension. According to the statement, Iran will not receive any previously frozen U.S. funds as part of the arrangement.

The announcement came via Truth Social at 15:33 UTC and was quickly echoed across major wire services. Trump described the ongoing negotiations as “nearly complete” with further talks expected in the coming days.

Market reaction was immediate: the U.S. Dollar strengthened against major peers, while Gold and Oil both dropped sharply as geopolitical risk premium evaporated. Brent crude fell as much as 4.2% in the minutes following the release.
