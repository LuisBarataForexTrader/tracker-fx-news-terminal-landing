FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Is Trading News Terminal really free? +
Yes. The core terminal — real-time news feed, economic calendar, live TV, squawk, sound alerts and watchlist — is completely free with no account required. A Pro tier with zero-delay real-time coverage and additional features is planned.
How fast is the news feed? +
The backend fetches from all 40+ RSS and API sources every 30 seconds and broadcasts new items to connected clients instantly via WebSocket. Most breaking news appears within 30–60 seconds of publication at the original source.
What is the Squawk feature? +
Squawk uses your browser's Web Speech API to read headlines aloud as they arrive — just like a professional trading desk squawk box. Enable it in one click, and the terminal will automatically read every new headline and highlight the card being spoken, so you never miss breaking news while watching charts.
Which sources does the terminal aggregate? +
Over 40 sources including Reuters, Bloomberg, CNBC, ForexLive, FXStreet, Wall Street Journal, MarketWatch, Benzinga, Axios, Financial Times, Politico, BBC Business, Kitco, CoinTelegraph, CoinDesk, DailyFX, Forex Factory, The Telegraph, TASS, and institutional sources like the Federal Reserve and ECB.
Does it work on mobile? +
Yes. The terminal is fully responsive and works on any modern mobile browser. For the best experience on mobile, add it to your home screen as a Progressive Web App (PWA support coming soon).
How does the economic calendar work? +
The economic calendar fetches 7 days of events with live countdown timers for upcoming releases. When actual data is published, the result is automatically injected into the News Feed with colour-coded positive/negative signals, and HIGH impact events trigger a sound alert and squawk.
