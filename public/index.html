FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about Trading News Terminal.

What is Trading News Terminal? +
Trading News Terminal is a professional real-time financial news platform for active traders. It aggregates live news from institutional sources across forex, macro, crypto, equities and commodities, classifies each item by market impact (HIGH, MEDIUM, LOW), and combines it with a live economic calendar, squawk box, TradingView charts and financial TV — all in one zero-clutter web interface.
What is the difference between Basic and Pro? +

Basic (free) includes: news feed with 2-minute delay, live economic calendar, TradingView charts, asset watchlist, smart search and multilingual interface.

Pro (€40/month) adds everything in Basic plus: Real-time zero-delay news feed — Macro & Bonds Hub (GDP, PMI, yields) — Earnings Calendar (EPS beat/miss live) — Central Banks Tracker (Fed, ECB, BOE, BOJ, SNB…) — Market Sentiment (risk-on / risk-off) — Market Screener (Forex, Stocks, Crypto, Indices, Commodities & Bonds) — Live Squawk (hands-free voice alerts) — 7 Live Financial TV channels — comprehensive source coverage — 100% ad-free — no upgrade prompts — priority support.

Is the Basic plan really free? +
Yes. The Basic plan is free forever — no credit card required, no trial period, no auto-upgrade. Simply create an account with your email address in under 30 seconds and get immediate access to the full terminal.
What is the Squawk feature? +
Squawk uses your browser's Web Speech API to read every new headline aloud as it arrives — just like a professional trading desk squawk box. Enable it with one click. The terminal highlights the card being read in real time, so you never miss breaking news while watching charts. Squawk is a Pro-only feature.
Which news sources does the terminal aggregate? +
We aggregate from hundreds of institutional and professional news sources across forex, macro, equities, crypto and commodities. We keep our source list confidential for competitive reasons. The Basic plan covers the highest-impact sources; the Pro plan unlocks full comprehensive coverage across every category and asset class.
Does Trading News Terminal have live charts? +
Yes. The terminal includes embedded TradingView charts for forex pairs (EUR/USD, GBP/USD, USD/JPY and more), cryptocurrencies (BTC, ETH, XRP), major indices (S&P 500, DAX, Nikkei, FTSE), metals and energy (Gold, Silver, WTI, Brent, Natural Gas), and agricultural commodities (Corn, Wheat, Soybeans, Coffee, Cocoa, Cotton). Charts are available on both Basic and Pro plans.
How does the economic calendar work? +
The economic calendar shows 7 days of upcoming events with live countdown timers, previous values and forecasts. When actual data is published, the result is automatically injected into the news feed with colour-coded beat/miss signals. HIGH impact events trigger an audio alert and Squawk reading (on Pro). The calendar covers all major currencies and economies globally.
Is Trading News Terminal a Bloomberg alternative? +
Yes. Bloomberg Terminal costs approximately €1,800 per month. Trading News Terminal Pro delivers real-time financial news, economic calendar, live squawk, 7 live TV channels and TradingView charts for €40/month. For independent and retail traders who need professional-grade market intelligence without the institutional price, TNT is the obvious Bloomberg alternative.
Does it work on mobile and tablet? +
Mobile and tablet support is coming soon. The terminal is currently optimised for desktop and laptop browsers. A fully native mobile experience is in development.
