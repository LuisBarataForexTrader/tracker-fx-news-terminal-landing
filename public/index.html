FAQ

Frequently asked questions

What is the difference between Basic and Pro? +
The Basic plan is free and gives you access to the core terminal: news feed, economic calendar, squawk, sound alerts, asset watchlist and smart search. The Pro plan adds real-time news delivery, comprehensive source coverage, live financial TV, and a fully ad-free experience with no upgrade prompts.
Is the Basic plan really free? +
Yes. The Basic plan is free forever — no credit card required. Just create an account with your email and password in under 30 seconds and you're in.
What is the Squawk feature? +
Squawk uses your browser's Web Speech API to read headlines aloud as they arrive — just like a professional trading desk squawk box. Enable it in one click, and the terminal will automatically read every new headline and highlight the card being spoken, so you never miss breaking news while watching charts.
Which news sources does the terminal use? +
We aggregate from a curated set of institutional and professional sources across forex, macro, equities, crypto and commodities. We keep our source list confidential for competitive reasons. The Basic plan covers the most impactful sources; Pro unlocks our full, comprehensive coverage.
Does it work on mobile? +
Yes. The terminal is fully responsive and works on any modern mobile browser. For the best experience on mobile, add it to your home screen as a Progressive Web App (PWA support coming soon).
How does the economic calendar work? +
The economic calendar fetches 7 days of events with live countdown timers for upcoming releases. When actual data is published, the result is automatically injected into the News Feed with colour-coded positive/negative signals, and HIGH impact events trigger a sound alert and squawk.
When will the Pro plan be available? +
The Pro plan is currently in development. If you want early access, contact us at hello@tradingnewsterminal.com and we'll notify you as soon as it launches.
