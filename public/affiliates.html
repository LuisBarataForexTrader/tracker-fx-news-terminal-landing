FAQ

Common questions

What counts as a "client"?▼
A client is someone who purchases an annual TNT Pro subscription (€360 + VAT) via your link. Simply registering without paying counts as a lead, not a client.
Do monthly subscriptions earn commission?▼
No. Commissions apply only to annual subscriptions (€360 + VAT). Monthly plans (€40/mo) are not included in the affiliate program.
How long does the referral cookie last?▼
30 days. If someone clicks your link and purchases within 30 days, the conversion is attributed to you — even if they register and return later.
When and how do tier upgrades happen?▼
The system automatically detects when you reach a tier threshold. Our team reviews and activates the upgrade — you'll receive an email with your new benefits (coupon, free Pro, etc.).
When are payouts processed?▼
You can request a payout from your dashboard at any time with a 7-day minimum interval between requests. We process within a few business days via bank transfer (IBAN/BIC) or Stripe.
What is the Tier 2 discount coupon?▼
At Tier 2 you receive a personal Stripe coupon giving your audience 20% off their first annual subscription. Share it publicly — it helps you convert leads into clients faster.
What is the Tier 3 dedicated landing page?▼
We create a custom page at tradingnewsterminal.com/your-name designed for your community. It includes your referral link and a branded introduction to TNT Pro.
Is there a minimum number of referrals to apply?▼
No minimum. Anyone with an audience of traders, investors, or financial content consumers is welcome to apply. We evaluate applications based on audience quality, not size.
