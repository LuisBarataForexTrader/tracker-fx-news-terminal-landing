TradingView is widely regarded as the best web-based charting platform for forex traders. How do I read candlestick charts?

A candlestick represents price action over a given time period. The body shows the open and close prices — a green or white body means the close was higher than the open (bullish candle); a red or black body means the close was lower (bearish candle). The wicks (thin lines extending above and below the body) show the high and low for the period. Key candlestick patterns — Doji, Hammer, Shooting Star, Bullish Engulfing, Morning Star — give traders signals about potential reversals or continuations.
